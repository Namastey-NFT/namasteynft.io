CURATION PROCESS

An unbiased framework was established by the Namastey NFT team which enabled the esteemed team of curators to rate the entries without any influence. There was a layer of process in between which assured that no personal details were passed to the curators who eventually rated the stylish entries solely on merit.

MINTING PROCESS

The 300 Artwork Collection will be available for Category-wise Blind Minting on a custom Smart Contract by NamasteyNFT for a fixed price of 0.25 ETH per NFT.

MINTING IS LIVE

The proceeds from the Sale will be divided among the artists & the NamasteyNFT Community Fund in the ratio of 80:20. i.e., 0.2 ETH to the artist & .05 ETH to NamasteyNFT community Fund, which will be used to further support new & emerging artists in the Web3 space. The secondary sales will directly go to the artist.
