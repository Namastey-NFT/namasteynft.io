FAQs

How many entries are allowed per artist?

Every artist can submit 1 artwork through our art submission Typeform.

Is it okay to submit already minted work?

No. The submitted artwork should have not been minted on any platform.

What is the last date for submission?

Submissions end on 25th April 2022, at 20:00 (8 PM) IST.

How many entries will be selected?

A total of 300 entries will be selected & we will be posting those 300 selected creations on the Instagram and Twitter profiles of ‘NamasteyNFT’ on 25th April 2022.
