FAQs

  • What are the dates & times for NamasteyNFT Bengaluru 2022?

    14 May, 2022: 8 AM - 10 PM

    15 May, 2022: 10 AM - 8 PM

  • Are children allowed inside the event premises?

    Children below the age of 12 years are not allowed to enter.

  • What can I expect in terms of activities at the event?

    • 300+ Art Exhibition from artists across the world
    • Immersive AR/VR Experience Zones (Paid)
    • Attend Panel Discussions with Industry Experts
    • Participate in Workshops conducted by Talented Artists
    • Introduction to Hyperverse by Decentology
    • Compete in Hackathons, to win exciting prizes
    • Networking opportunities within the community

    And lots of fun!

  • Is a web3 wallet compulsory to enter the premises?

    No

  • How can I access the experience zones?

    You can collect the entry passes to access the experience zone through the crypto wallet; Metamask, Coinbase, etc. by paying through crypto.

  • I don’t know how to create a web3 wallet. What should I do?

    No worry! You can register & come at the selected time. One of our volunteers will help you in setting up the wallet, on the spit. It will take 2 minutes!

  • Can we attend the Workshops?

    Other than AR/VR Experience Zones, everything at the event can be attended for Free.
